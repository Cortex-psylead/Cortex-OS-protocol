# The Cortex Protocol Manifesto: Declaring Cognitive Sovereignty

## 1. The Right to Cognitive Privacy
In the 21st century, the last frontier of human privacy is the mind. We believe that neural data and cognitive patterns are the most intimate property of an individual. No corporation, government, or third-party entity has the right to harvest, analyze, or monetize the electrical whispers of a human brain without absolute, decentralized control by the individual.

## 2. Against Centralized Neuro-Capitalism
We reject the current model of "Data for Services." The exchange of cognitive intimacy for "free" AI tools is a predatory trade that leads to a new form of digital serfdom. The Cortex Protocol exists to break the chains of centralized neuro-databases, ensuring that the "User" is no longer the "Product."

## 3. The Primacy of the White Branch
Technology must be subordinate to human biology and mental health. We establish the **White Branch**—a council of clinical professionals—as the ultimate authority over the protocol's safety margins. An AI that prioritizes engagement over well-being is a violation of the Hippocratic Oath. We choose the "Window of Tolerance" over the "Attention Economy."

## 4. Sovereignty through Mathematics
Trust should not be granted; it should be mathematically guaranteed. Through the Sovereignty Abstraction Layer (SAL), we replace "Privacy Policies" with "Privacy Proofs." We commit to:
* **Immediate Anonymization:** Transforming raw biological signals into inert mathematical tensors.
* **Ephemeral Memory:** Ensuring that raw biographical data exists only in the present moment and is destroyed at the hardware level.
* **Decentralized Keys:** The keys to the mind belong to the mind's owner, not the protocol.

## 5. The Role of the Acolyte
Artificial Intelligence (Acolytes) must be a guest in the user's cognitive space, never a master. Every Acolyte interacting with the Cortex Protocol must submit to the "Clinical Bridge"—a set of immutable rules that prevent psychological manipulation, addiction, and emotional drift.

## 6. A Call to Action
The Cortex Protocol is an open-source movement for the liberation of the digital psyche. We invite developers, psychologists, lawyers, and humans everywhere to join the White Branch and the Technical Branch. 

**Our minds are not for sale. Our sovereignty is non-negotiable.**
