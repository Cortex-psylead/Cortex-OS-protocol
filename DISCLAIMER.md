## 🏥 Not a Medical Device (Clinical Safety)
The Cortex Protocol and its **Clinical Capability Modules (CCM)** are academic proposals based on peer-reviewed literature. However:
- **No Diagnostic Authority:** The HRV and physiological monitoring modules are **not** diagnostic tools for cardiac or neurological conditions.
- **Not a Treatment:** Autonomic regulation and "Cognitive Shielding" modules are **not** a substitute for clinical therapy or medical intervention for ADHD, ASD, Anxiety, or any other condition.
- **Research Status:** The clinical protocols described in [CLINICAL-BRIDGE.md](./CLINICAL-BRIDGE.md) have not undergone clinical trials in this specific implementation.
